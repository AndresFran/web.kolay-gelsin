STAFF

Lorena Delgado

Founder


Lorena came to Türkiye the first time in 2001 and fell in love with Turkish culture and language.

After getting a degree in psychology and undergo the Language Learning Advisor training she dedicated years to coach people in their Turkish Learning journey.

Kolay Gelsin was founded in 2022 with the desire to help people get immerse into Turkish culture through their language.

Seeing people learn this language in a short period of time while thriving in a host culture is one of the biggest rewards for her.
