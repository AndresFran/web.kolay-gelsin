Hello Students

Welcome to Kolay Gelsing

We are a language center dedicated to help you learn the Turkish language in a simple and easy way with an innovative method.

Join Us Now!

1. CONNECT

Listen-and-Do games, Interact about Here-and-Noe, Focus on Sound, Focus on Form games, Sight Words.

800 words
100 hours

2. EMERGE

Describe pictures, Build stories, How-tu-sequences, Discuss (knead) recording, Role-play, Small talk, Read and write about what you can already discuss.

+1200 = 2000 words
+150 = 250 hours

3. KNOWABLE

Discuss shared experiences, familiar stories, people and places. Scripts of Life (normal routines). Discuss (knead) recordings, Record Self for Feedback. Read and write about what you can already discuss.

+2500 = 4500 words
+250 = 500 hours

4. DEEPER RELATIONSHIPS

Discuss interviews, Discuss detailed observation, Discuss life stories, Discuss (knead) recordings, Record Self for Feedback. Read and write about what you can already discuss.

+3000 = 7500 words
+500 = 1000 hours

5. WIDER UNDERSTANDING

Discuss recording of native-to-native talk. Read and write about what you can already discuss.

+1500 = 9000 words
+500 = 1500 hours

6. EVER GROWING

Community-sustained learning.

Always more words.
You are able to learn from everyone.
