A TURKISH LEARNING PROGRAM

To achieve a thorough comprehension and fluency in Turkish, it's important to choose a structured language program with well-planned classes that make the most of your time, and are facilitated by experienced Turkish language nurturers and a language coach to support your learning.

Our program provides a structured approach that allows you to learn at your own pace, with access to all the necessary resources to improve your comprehension and enhance your communication skills as you progress towards fluency in spoken language.

At Kolay Gelsin we do not provide a “language helper” only, we make sure that our program is applied for every Phase. We call our students Participants because we believe that learning a language involves learning about the culture as well. We constantly remind our participants of the importance of engaging in life and human relationships, not just learning a language. Our teachers are more than just assistants, we call them Nurturers as they introduce participants to the language and culture so that there is a respectful interaction between the participant and the culture they are living in.

Each class is well-structured so that the participant can get the most out of their lesson and our Nurturers get frequent training to apply every aspect of our approach thus getting the best results.

When a baby is born and immerse in the culture and language that surrounds them, they experience the sounds and words for an average of two years. At Kolay Gelsin, we make this process shorter, where the participant becomes familiar with the language before starting to speak it.

We approach learning through comprehension, our approach has nothing to do with grammatical rules but with training language comprehension, where common sense is used to form our own sentences.
