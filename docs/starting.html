Starting with Turkish
In a matter of months, our Turkish participants have progressed from having no knowledge to achieving fluency.
Why is Kolay Gelsin different to other centers?
What sets us apart from other language programs is our unique approach.
Our program is different.
Numerous language programs encourage you to "study Turkish" by relying on visual learning. However, Kolay Gelsin's introductory Turkish program takes a different approach, emphasizing auditory learning - just as you did when learning your first language. By hearing and observing, then imitating and practicing, you will develop an instinctive understanding of the language. This immersive method allows you to learn from locals and to speak like them. That’s why we use recordings in all of our lessons for you to take home and study.
We also make learning a language fun.
We believe that when students enjoy the learning process, they're more likely to stay motivated and make faster progress. Language learning has to be relational and practical to build the fluency we want to achieve.
Our program is highly effective.
Our primary focus is to ensure a familiar understanding of the Turkish language before attempting to speak it. That's why our program is structured in such a way that comprehension is established even before you start speaking. Having a good comprehension is crucial to language learning and is more important than accumulating irrelevant knowledge for everyday use. That's why we prioritize language that is relevant to you, so that it can be acquired more naturally.
So if you're looking for a language program that's truly unique, look no further. Our program is different, fun, and effective – and we're confident that you'll love it.
